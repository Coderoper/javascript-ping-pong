About Me

I'm 30 years old, I live in Seattle and I 've bee here for a little over 2 years. I love it! I grew up in Oklahoma, home of tornado alley. I graduated from Oklahoma State University in 2012 where I received a bachelor's of science in mechanical engineering. After college I went to work at Powell Industries in Houston where we designed and manufactured switchgear. When the oil crisis hit I lost my job, so I decided to move to Seattle. I found another engineering job at Electroimpact in Mukilteo I worked there for 2 years and was laid off in January. I decided that I wanted to make a career change and chose coding because I like the logic, the problem solving aspect and the career oppurtunities that are available with the skillset.
